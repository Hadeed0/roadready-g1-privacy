This Privacy Policy describes how [Your Name/Company Name] ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our RoadReady G1 Ontario mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve the App, manage your account, and offer features like progress tracking and premium access. The types of information we collect include:

Account Information:

  • Anonymous Usage: If you use the App without creating a permanent account, we use Firebase Authentication to generate a temporary anonymous user ID to save your progress locally (e.g., quiz scores, chapter completion, settings). This ID is not directly linked to your personal identity unless you choose to link your account.
  • Linked Accounts: If you choose to link your anonymous account or sign up for a permanent account using Email/Password, Google Sign-In, or Sign in with Apple, we collect the necessary information provided by you or the authentication provider:
    • Firebase Authentication: Your email address (for email/password), unique Firebase User ID (UID).
    • Google Sign-In: Information authorized by you via Google, potentially including your name, email address, and Google User ID. This is used to create or link your Firebase account.
    • Sign in with Apple: Information authorized by you via Apple, potentially including your name and email address (which may be a private relay address). This is used to create or link your Firebase account.
  • We store this account information securely using Firebase Authentication.

App Usage and Progress Data:

  • Stored Locally & Potentially Synced (linked to your UID in Firestore): Quiz answers, quiz scores (last score, high score), chapter completion status, last read chapter, last visited quiz level, study streak data, mock exam history, favorited questions, incorrect answers list, language preference, theme preference, audio playback settings (speed, repeat count). This data is stored against your Firebase User ID (anonymous or permanent) in Firebase Firestore to provide features like progress tracking and review modes.

Subscription and Purchase Information:

  • Managed by RevenueCat & App Stores: If you purchase a premium subscription, the purchase process is handled by the respective app store (Apple App Store or Google Play Store) and our subscription management provider, RevenueCat. We do not directly collect or store your credit card or payment details.
  • RevenueCat Data: RevenueCat receives your purchase information from the app store and associates it with your current Firebase User ID (provided via Purchases.logIn). We receive entitlement status (e.g., whether "premium" is active) and potentially subscription expiration dates from RevenueCat to unlock premium features in the App.

Device and Technical Information (Collected by Third-Party Services):

  • Our third-party service providers (Firebase, RevenueCat, Google Play Services, Apple Services) may automatically collect certain technical information necessary for the App's functionality, security, and improvement. This can include:
    • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID, device ID for push notifications if implemented).
    • IP address (used for general location, fraud prevention, service delivery).
    • Operating system version, device model, app version.
    • Crash logs and performance data (e.g., via Firebase Crashlytics/Performance Monitoring, if used).
    • General usage analytics (e.g., screens visited, features used, session duration via Firebase Analytics, if used) often in an aggregated or anonymized form.
